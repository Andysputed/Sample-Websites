CODING CHALLENGE EVENT

These are the information for the Coding Challenge event which will be held on:

  • Date:12thOctober 2023
  • Time:9:30 AM
  • Location:Nairobi Uhuru Park Grounds
    •

The Coding Challenge event wil be a life changing event full of talented individuals who are urged to come showercase their coding abilities upto their full potential. The event will be East African wide which means every one with coding talent within East Africa are welcomed.


Why Participate:

The event places a strong emphasis on fostering innovativeness, enabling valuable networking, and encouraging collaborative efforts. The event offers cash rewards for the top 10 teams, opportunities for project incubation contracts with our sponsors, and certificates to acknowledge all participants' achievements


Abstract Submission Guidelines:

  1. Select a topic from the provided options and create groups of 2-5 members each. Note that a student can lead in one group and participate as a member in another group, with participation limited to two groups per student.
  2. The deadline for registering nd submitting your Abstract is Saturday, 30thSeptember 2023
